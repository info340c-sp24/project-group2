Project: Topic Proposal

Problem/Data Domain

Effective communication and organization are imperative for success and efficiency among student organizations. Registered Student Organizations (RSOs) often face challenges in coordinating meetings, sharing updates, and collaborating with members in a centralized and unified platform. Currently, many organizations use Slack for communication purposes, but it lacks specialized features tailored to the needs of these RSOs. The gap in functionality hinders efficient management and communication within student organizations.

Some previous attempts to address this issue have involved utilizing generic communication tools like Slack or GroupMe. While these platforms offer basic messaging capabilities and media uploads, they lack integration with organizational features such as event scheduling, task management, and member engagement tracking. This often causes RSO leaders and members to use multiple platforms to manage various aspects of their organization, which can cause difficulty in efficient communication. By addressing these issues and providing a dedicated platform designed specifically for RSO management, we can streamline organizational tasks, improve communication, and enhance internal and external communication. This project aims to leverage features and tools from existing technologies while implementing tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of RSOs, empowering student leaders to run their organizations in an effective and efficient manner.

App Description

This application targets students at universities who are part of Registered Student Organizations (RSOs). RSOs do not receive as much recognition, and therefore resources and funding from the University, in comparison to sports teams. This would allow minority groups to receive management resources to better support their progression and achievements as RSOs, who work toward promoting student involvement in extracurricular activities.

Solution Impact

This app addresses the inherent challenges faced by RSOs by providing a centralized platform with tailored features, which streamlines the management process, enhances communication efficiency, and fosters internal and external collaboration. The implementation of key features such as the scheduling feature allows RSOs to coordinate meetings and events, eliminating the need for manual scheduling and ultimately reducing logistical confusion. The messaging feature facilitates internal communication within RSOs such that leaders are able to reach out to members of their team or organizations to communicate important information. It also facilitates communication with other RSOs for collaborative purposes. Lastly, the reminder feature ensures that members stay informed and engaged by sending automated reminders for scheduled meetings or events, as well as reminders on unread messages. This solution directly tackles the inefficiencies caused by relying on multiple platforms for different organizational tasks. It also ensures accessibility to all RSOs, including those representing minority groups, for equal access to essential management resources. This fosters inclusivity and empowers all student organizations with tools to succeed. The practical impact of this system ultimately lies in its ability to support the growth of RSOs, contributing to a more engaged campus community.
